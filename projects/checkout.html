Understanding Payment Flow

Taking payments online is scary when you're new. "What if I mess up someone's credit card?" So I built this sandbox to learn the flow without the risk.

Turns out, Stripe handles the scary parts. You never touch raw card numbers. You just orchestrate the checkout flow and handle the results.
